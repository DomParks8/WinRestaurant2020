The Love of Food and Travel

Food

My love of food started at an early age. Growing up in the south I was used to home cooked soul food meals made with love from my amazing grandmother. The fact that I grew up eating the food of the best cook know to man has spoiled my palate to only accept the best of the best. Take my suggestions and follow me on this journey to the most amazing meals that you've ever had.

Travel

Later on in life I discovered that there are actually places outside of Concord NC, and I was determined to see them all. I started by taking weekend roadtrips and eventually that evolved into taking week long trips out of the country. Once I caught the travel bug, I wass never able to shake it. It became a goal of mine to travel as much as possible and soon after I began to combine my love of both food and travel.
